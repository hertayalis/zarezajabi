Julia Barretto Life

Julia Barretto was born on March 10, 1997, in Marikina, Philippines, to renowned actors Dennis Padilla and Marjorie Barretto. She has two siblings, Claudia Barretto and Leon Barretto, along with two half-sisters, Erich and Dani Barretto, from her mother’s side. Julia completed her education at Miriam College and St. Paul College, focusing on her career in acting thereafter.

Julia Barretto Education

Julia completed her grade school education at Miriam College in March 2011, followed by high school at St. Paul College in Molave Pasig in March 2015.
