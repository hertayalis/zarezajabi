Latto Bio (Wiki)

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Who is Latto?

Latto is a popular American rapper who is commonly known by her name as Mulatto or Big Latto who is from Atlanta, Georgia.  She is a well-known personality who has been recognized for the reality television series name The Rap Game and also she has won the show’s first season. She is nominated for Best New Hip Hop Artist at the 2020 BET Hip Hop Awards.

Latto: Age, Family, Siblings

Alyssa Michelle Stephens who is Latto was born on December 22, 1998, in Columbus, Ohio, US. Stephens holds the nationality of American citizenship and belongs to the multiracial descent of African-American, Indian, Irish, German, and Welsh descent.

She was born to Misti (nee Stephens) Pitts who was a white mother and Shayne Pitts who was a black father. So, she was bullied in school for her light-skinned which inspired her to adopt the stage name called Miss Mulatto. She moved to Georgia when she was just 2 years old. She also has siblings named Kay and Brooklyn.

In which school did Latto attend?

As she is raised in Clayton County where she went to the school named Lovejoy High School. Now she has graduated from the school but hasn’t said any details regarding her university name.

Latto: Professional life, Career

She decided to become a rapper at the age of 10. After that, she began to write rap music and took part in drag racing.

In 2016 she decided to take part as a contestant on the reality series The Rap Game. From this, she was known by her stage name Miss Mulatto and also won the competition. She also got an offer from Dupri with So So Def Records but she turned them down as money was not enough.

After this, she released her first single in February 2016 that was No More Talking. Her second mixtape was released in April 2017  Latto Let Em Know. She had worked with many singers in that year like 2 Chainz, Future, Janelle Monae, and Jacob Latimore.

She then changed her name to Mulatto in 2018 and released the self-titled mixtape of the same name that year.

In January 2019, she released the song  Bitch from da Souf.  In the same year of  June, she also released her second extended play Big Latto which was preceded by Bitch from da Souf. Her single breakout reached 95 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

In 2020, she signed a contract with RCA Records. On April 23 she released her single No Hook. That was her first promotion single later in some month she released her second promotional single He Says She Say on May 21. This year she released many singles and many kinds of music. She was also featured on G-Eazy single Down. Her song peaked at number 44 on the US Billboard 2000.

In 2021 there was an announcement that Latto was changing her name to Mulatto there was controversy over that as that term was known to be a colorist.  In the year that February Latto was named MTV’s Global Push Artist of the month.

On September 24 she released her lead single that was named Big Energy and it was her second studio album.

Some of the television series she has acted in are The Rap Game, Love & Hip Hop: Miami, Queen of Stylez, and Wild’n Out.
