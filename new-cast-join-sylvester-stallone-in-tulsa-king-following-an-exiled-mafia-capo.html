Watch The Trailer For Tulsa King Starring Sylvester Stallone

June 2024 · 3 minute read

The Paramount+ original series from Taylor Sheridan and Terence Winter premieres Nov. 13th.

Get a closer look at Sylvester Stallone in his first TV series leading role in the official trailer for Tulsa King, premiering Sunday, Nov. 13 exclusively on Paramount+. 

READ: Taylor Sheridan’s CIA Series Lioness Adds To Cast

The trailer shows off some of the previously announced talent joining Stallone in Taylor Sheridan and Terence Winter's Paramount+ original series. 

Tulsa King follows New York mafia capo Dwight "The General" Manfredi, played by Sylvester Stallone. Just as he's released from prison after 25 years, Dwight is unceremoniously exiled by his boss to set up shop in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Realizing that his mob family may not have his best interests in mind, Dwight slowly builds a "crew" from a group of unlikely characters to help him establish a new criminal empire in a place that to him might as well be another planet.

Dana Delaney (China Beach) will appear alongside Stallone as Margaret, an affluent and influential equestrian and owner of a sprawling horse farm and animal preserve. She is a formidable presence and a trustee of the Annie Oakley Society.

Previously announced cast includes:

— Andrea Savage (I'm Sorry)
— Martin Starr (Silicon Valley)
— Max Casella (The Tender Bar) will play Armand Truisi
— Domenick Lombardozzi (Billions) will play Charles "Chickie" Invernizzi
— Vincent Piazza (Boardwalk Empire) will play Vince Antonacci
— Jay Will (The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel) will play Tyson
— A.C. Peterson (Superman & Lois)
— Garrett Hedlund (The United States vs. Billie Holiday)

Tulsa King will be executive produced by Academy Award® nominee Sheridan, Academy Award® nominee and Emmy Award® winner Winter, along with Stallone, David C. Glasser, Ron Burkle, Bob Yari, David Hutkin and Allen Coulter. Braden Aftergood is also set to executive produce. 

Tulsa King is the latest addition to Sheridan’s growing Paramount+ empire, which includes the service’s top two performing original series, 1883 and Mayor of Kingstown, as well as the upcoming series Lioness, starring Zoe Saldana; 1883: The Bass Reeves Story; Land Man with Billy Bob Thornton; and 1923, a new installment in the Dutton family chronicles that takes place following the end of WWI and the start of Prohibition. The series will be produced by MTVE Entertainment Studios and 101 Studios.

Paramount Network will host a linear airing of Tulsa King’s first two episodes on Sunday, Nov. 20, immediately following a new episode of Yellowstone. All remaining episodes will be available to stream weekly on Sundays, exclusively on Paramount+. Tulsa King will also premiere on Paramount+ in Canada on Nov. 13, followed by a premiere in Australia and The UK on Nov. 14. The series will air later this year on Paramount+ in Latin America and in additional territories upon the launch of the service.
