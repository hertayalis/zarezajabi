Who is Eazy Es daughter from Growing Up Hip Hop? | Meet ReeMarkable on Instagram!

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The reality TV series “Growing Up Hip Hop Atlanta” provides a glimpse into the lives of music artists chasing their dreams, and one of the standout stars is none other than Henree Cherron Wright. Professionally known as ReeMarkable, she is not only a rapper and singer but also the daughter of the legendary Eazy E. In this article, we’ll get to know ReeMarkable better and explore her journey in the world of music.

Discovering Eazy E’s Daughter

Henree Cherron Wright, known by her stage name ReeMarkable, is a 27-year-old rapper and singer making her mark on the music scene. Born in Atlanta, Georgia, she entered this world during Eazy E’s relationship with Kelly Faultersack Robinson. However, she was raised by her mother in Decatur, showing that her roots run deep in the South’s vibrant music culture.

A Musical Legacy

ReeMarkable’s journey into music has been both influenced and inspired by her father, the late Eazy E. While some may assume she ventured into music simply because of her iconic father, ReeMarkable emphasized that her passion for music goes beyond her family legacy. Born after Eazy E began his own musical odyssey, she has forged her unique path in the industry. She is determined to earn respect and recognition as an individual artist, showcasing her distinct talent and style.

From Church Choir to the Stage

ReeMarkable’s musical journey began in the church choir, a testament to the influence of her great grandmother who encouraged her to explore her vocal talents. It’s here that she discovered her love for singing, laying the foundation for her future career. Her dedication and commitment to music have led her to where she is today, following in the footsteps of her father while carving out her own musical legacy.

Life Beyond the Mic

ReeMarkable’s life extends beyond her music career. On her Instagram, she offers a glimpse into her latest musical projects, providing fans with updates on her creative endeavors. Additionally, she shares her experiences as a mother to her son Cobe, who entered her life when she was just 16 years old. In her Instagram bio, she proudly dubs herself “The Lion Queen,” a fitting title for a fierce and talented artist.

Vibrant and Versatile

ReeMarkable’s presence is not limited to her music alone. She has embraced various hair colors and styles, from bright orange to pink, blue, and purple—all at once! Her bold and versatile appearance mirrors her fearless approach to her artistry, unafraid to stand out and make a statement.

As fans continue to follow ReeMarkable’s journey on “Growing Up Hip Hop Atlanta,” they can witness the evolution of an artist who is determined to leave her unique mark on the world of music.

Conclusion

ReeMarkable, also known as Henree Cherron Wright, is more than just Eazy E’s daughter; she is a talented artist in her own right. With a passion for music that runs deep, she is forging her path in the music industry while embracing her role as a mother. Her journey, showcased on “Growing Up Hip Hop Atlanta,” is one of determination, individuality, and undeniable talent. As she continues to captivate audiences, we eagerly anticipate the next chapters of her remarkable career.
